About Me

As a photographer, I can subconsciously communicate emotions and feelings hidden randomly in life compositions. Many consider me, an artistic poet who uses photos as his vehicle. I have traveled all over the world, including Dubai, Chile, Japan, and Alaska, in the name of photography. My photographs are meant to be viewed on two levels — surface and interior.

Unbeknownst to the observer, a thought crossed their minds as they looked at Saya's photos. This breeze comes in and is felt as an emotion, perhaps a lost memory—perhaps a song carried by thoughts of lost love. It's all captured in one brief moment and that's the reason why his photos make an impact.
